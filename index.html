Uttarann

Uttarann is a Spiritual Foundation comprised of Shreejashram and Shree Aum Charitable Trust.

It is a space for the seeker to find their own truth. It is a space for the devotee to lose themselves in devotion. It is a space for the one who wants to serve to find their purpose.

Shreejashram

Shreejashram is a Charitable Trust founded by Shree Guru Maashree. It is a sanctuary for spiritual seekers and a center for selfless service.

Located in the serene Sahyadri hills, the Ashram offers a conducive environment for meditation, self-reflection, and spiritual growth.
