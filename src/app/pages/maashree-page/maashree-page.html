Her Life Message

The prime message Maashree conveys is Love, evoking compassion and devotion aimed at the unification of souls. Under Her divine shelter, many have smoothed their lives by making it more peaceful and delightful.

Maashree speaks of the Life Path, to seek out the truth, and to cultivate reverential faith and total surrender towards the sublime beauty of the Almighty's presence, and the power bestowed within us.
