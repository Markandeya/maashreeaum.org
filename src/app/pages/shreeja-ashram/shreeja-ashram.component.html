WELCOME TO

Shreejaashram

Shreejaashram is a peaceful and sacred sanctuary nestled in the picturesque locale of the Sahyadri ranges of Maharashtra, located approximately 165 km from Mumbai via the Mumbai-Nashik highway.

The atmosphere of the Ashram is highly conducive to spiritual practice and inward meditation, as it works as the geological epicenter of Shree Guru Maashree's mission. We host various celebrations like Guru Purnima, Shiv Pranaam (Shiv Ratri), Purnahati Yajna, Uma Usha Yajna, Durga Puja Utsav as well as other annual Retreats at Shreejaashram.

Nearest airport: Mumbai (124 km/ 3.5 hours). Taxi to the Ashram costs about ₹3,500–4,000.

Nearest railhead: Kasara (30 km/45 mins). Serviced by local trains on the Main Line from Mumbai CST. From Kasara any ST bus heading for Jawhar or Wada will drop you off at Khodala.

Several buses run from Thane to Nashik via Wada, halt at Khodala.

Take NH3 till Jawhar Phata via Thane and Shahapur. Turn left off the high way onto the road to Khodala.
