DAANAM

In Service of the Divine

We encourage donations in support of our various initiatives, be it for the welfare and well-being of animals, the upkeep and development of Shreejaashram, or the publication of Maashree's discourses in a variety of formats on our Shree Aum Website.

With the support of our donors, Uttarann carries out initiatives like Tribal Welfare Programs in rural Maharashtra and Stand For Animals in major cities of India. At Shreejaashram, we host meditation retreats, celebrate important pujas and conduct annual yajnas (Purnahati and Ama Usha) in the Grace and Guidance of SHREE GURU MAASHREE.

Shreejaashram Charitable Trust ensures all funds are utilized transparently and effectively for its projects. Maashree's discourses and publications are accessible through the Shree Aum Website.
