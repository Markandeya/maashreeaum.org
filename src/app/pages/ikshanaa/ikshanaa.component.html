iKSHANAA

Discover Your True Self

You have worked hard this year. Achieved your targets at work. Supported your child's dreams, given quality life to your family.
What about your SELF?

iKSHANAA is the way to see your SELF. It is a 3 days - 3 nights in-person programme to learn Applied Spirituality in everyday life. You can discover how to quiet the constant chatter of thoughts and worries in your mind. Thus, learning the secret to be free of anxiety and reduce stress in your everyday life.

Programme Highlights
  • • Bhavasan and Meditation sessions
  • • Introspection and Self-reflection
  • • Simple methods to integrate spirituality into everyday life
  • • A nurturing environment to explore your true self
Venue: Shreejaashram, Khodala, Maharashtra
Dates: 18th - 21st February 2026
