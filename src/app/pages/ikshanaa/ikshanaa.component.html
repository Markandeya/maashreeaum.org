OVERVIEW

What is iKshanaa?

In a world that moves faster than ever, we often lose touch with our center — pulled between responsibilities, ambitions, expectations, and an unending stream of noise.

Yet beneath all of this, there remains a quiet call within each of us: to pause, to re-align, and to truly listen to ourself with clarity, and inner steadiness.

iKshanaa, a 3-day 3-night residential programme at the serene and blessed Shreejaashram, Khodala, Maharashtra, is an offering to help rediscover that inner compass, our True North. Set amidst the silence of nature, this programme invites you to step away from the external rush and gently turn inward.

What iKshanaa Offers

A deeper understanding of your inner landscape

Explore the emotional, mental, and psychological pressures of modern living — and learn how they silently shape your choices.

Movement from restlessness to mindfulness

Shift from distraction to focus, from noise to clarity, from emotional turbulence to inner ease.

Practical tools for inner peace

Simple yet powerful techniques rooted in traditional wisdom, designed to bring you back to balance in everyday life.

Seeing your own life path clearly

Recognize your patterns, your strengths, your resistances — and the deeper direction your life is inviting you toward.

Programme Highlights

  • Bhaavasan and Meditation sessions
  • Introspection and Self-reflection
  • Simple methods to integrate spirituality into everyday life
  • A nurturing environment to explore your true self

This programme is relevant to any working professional or householder who feels the world has become too loud, and wishes to return to silence, sincerity, and inner direction.

What You Will Take Back

  • Renewed inner steadiness
  • A clearer understanding of life’s challenges
  • Mindful tools to navigate stress and uncertainty
  • A strengthened inner compass
  • Ability to prioritize what truly matters
  • A sense of expansion and spaciousness within
  • A deeper connection to your own path and purpose

"Most importantly, you will return with an inner quiet — a space you can revisit anytime, anywhere."
