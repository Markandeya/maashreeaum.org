UTTARANN NEWSLETTER

October - December 2025

by sadhaks of Uttarann

OCTOBER 2025

Durga Puja & Sacred Festivities

Shreejaashram's ambiance transformed in October as Sadhaks immersed themselves in Durga Puja festivities. With the guidance of SHREE GURU, Sadhaks performed Pitru Tarpan followed by Ghat Sthapana, initiating the celebrations, with Alpanas and intricate Ek-chala decorations adorning the grounds.

Each day of Durga Puja brought forth SHREE GURU's profound messages, inspiring introspection and self-reflection. The Dhaak's rhythmic beats stirred Sadhaks to dance in ecstasy, surrounding SHREE GURU. Ghat Visarjan in the Yagna Bhumi, on the last day of Navaratri, Dussehra, marked a poignant reminder of the triumph of virtue over vice.

This was followed by Jagaran Purnima, widely observed as the day of Kojagiri Laxmi Puja across many regions, symbolizing the awakening of inner light or the viveka, the higher discernment. As SHREE GURU says, "Through the practice of dhyan (meditation) and by embracing suddha (sacredness), satya (truth), and sattva (purity), the mind can transcend and merge into the cosmic state."

NOVEMBER 2025

Winter Bells & Spiritual Sessions

The gentle chime of winter bells arrived early this November at Shreejaashram, ushering in a season of devotion and joy. Beginning with the celebration of Raas Purnima and Guru Nanak Jayanti, on one side yagna was performed at the Kochin Ashram, filling the atmosphere with auspicious vibrations. Simultaneously, devotees at Shreejaashram were blessed with the live discourse of SHREE GURU, whose words illuminated hearts and minds with wisdom and peace.

This month, the disciples of Shreejaashram were invited by Udaya International School, Gorakhpur, to conduct a comprehensive session on Bhavasan, Pranayam, and Meditation. The program spanned across 3 days having 12 sessions and was attended by school students, along with the faculty of pre-primary and primary sections. The presence of the Principal Sir further graced the occasion, adding to its significance.

The sessions were not only marked by active participation but also by meaningful interaction. Students engaged deeply, raising questions on topics such as managing anger, coping with stress, and handling various pressures at home and in society. These discussions created a nurturing environment where practical guidance met youthful curiosity, making the initiative both impactful and memorable.

DECEMBER 2025

Bhagwatam Path & Foundation Day

December marked a vibrant period of festivities in UTTARANN, commemorating Bhagwatam Path at SHREEJAASHRAM, Khodala. The 5-day event featured recitations from the Bhagwat Gita in Malayalam, meticulously translated to Hindi, with in-depth explanations of each sloka and mantra. The discourse captivated the audience, fostering a deep connection with the ancient scriptures, and inspiring spiritual growth.

This was followed by UTTARANN's 19th Foundation Day celebrations on December 25th, held in the revered presence of SHREE GURU, adding to the joyous atmosphere.

The year concluded with a meaningful 2-day Medical Camp, providing essential healthcare services to the community, and a heartwarming initiative – distribution of blankets and nutritious food to over 300 villagers in nearby schools and communities, spreading warmth and kindness.
