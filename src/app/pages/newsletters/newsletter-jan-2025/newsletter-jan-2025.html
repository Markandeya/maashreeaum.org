JUNE 2025

In June, few sadhaks of Uttaran were bestowed with the divine grace to participate in a Meditation retreat in Tawang from 6th-14th June.

In Tawang, SHREE GURU took residence in a resort nestled in the valley of Tawang, called Timilo, which means Holy Slope. On the peak of this hill sits the Tawang Monastery. Sadhaks visited this monastery along with SHREE GURU, the next day. SHREE GURU was seeking something, that led HER Divine being to embark on this journey to Tawang. On further enquiry, the hotel manager informed about the presence of a remote Monastery on a hill, where people do not venture without caution. Visiting that place requires one to observe austerities and restrict their mind, body and what they eat for a duration of atleast three days.

On the second day of the retreat, SHREE GURU along with the sadhaks embarked on the journey to this remote Monastery through an expanse of dense forest, under the guidance of a local guide. SHREE GURU walked on foot on this uncertain terrain through the dense forest to reach the Monastery - Krompten Gonpa.
