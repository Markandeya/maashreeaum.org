The terrain rose and fell with natural grace, creating a breathtaking landscape that felt both peaceful and powerful. As we walked through the area, taking in its wide open spaces and quiet beauty, the Sadhaks were filled with awe. The sheer scale of the land amazed everyone ‚Äî step after step, we covered the hills, feeling both the strength of the earth beneath us and the silence of nature around us. MARCH 2025

The celebration begins with SHIV PRANAAM, as sadhaks reverently offer holy water upon the SHILA, guided by the divine presence of SHREE GURU.

Through this sacred ritual, they behold the embodiment of SHIVA through SHREE GURU. The entire bhumi transforms into a celestial realm, elevated by the Guru's presence, the resonance of sacred mantras, and the depth of DHYANAM.

Thereafter unfolds the sacred celebration of ANANDA UTSAV, held in honor of SHREE GURU's divine birthday.

On this auspicious day, SHREE GURU imparts profound insights into the depth and intensity of sadhana. In the Guru's presence, sadhaks come forward to share their heartfelt experiences and inner transformations encountered along the PATH.

Under the divine guidance of SHREE GURU, sadhaks immerse in deeper sadhana and are elevated into a sublime state of ANANDA, transcending ordinary experience.

DOL UTSAV unfolds in a vibrant cascade of colors, lovingly derived from nature's own palette.

Sadhaks gently apply these sacred hues upon one another with deep LOVE and RESPECT, symbolizing the infusion of DIVINE consciousness into the mind. As SHREE GURU gracefully scatters colors and rose petals with HER own hands, the entire space becomes sanctified‚Äîbathed in beauty and devotion with the resonance of the sacred GURU AUM mantra sung by sadhaks along with SHREE GURU.

Walking among the sadhaks, SHREE GURU envelops each one in HER DIVINE LOVE, and in that sacred moment, sadhaks behold the presence of SHREE KRISHNA through SHREE GURU.

The atmosphere transforms into the divine essence of Vrindavan, echoing with bliss and spiritual ecstasy.

MAY 2025

With deepest reverence to SHRI GURU, the Buddha Purnima celebration at the ashram unfolds with profound intensity. In the sacred presence of SHRI GURU, not only does Nature seem to deepen in stillness, but the mind also enters a heightened state of awareness.

SHRI GURU reminds us that even with suffering, the mind often seeks comfort in the ordinary, becoming content with superficial engagements while neglecting deeper contemplation. However, it is through suffering that we are nudged to transcend the mundane and awaken to higher truths. Here is where an evolved mind transforms into the state of BUDDHA.

One powerful insight from SHRI GURU'S discourse has stirred the hearts of all disciples, inspiring a shift in perception and opening the door to a more expansive understanding.
